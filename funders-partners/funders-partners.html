Funders and Partners

AIMS RIC is funded through public-private partnerships which combine government, foundations, corporations, partner institutions, and individuals who share in the goal of unlocking Africa’s scientific potential to foster major discovery. We are grateful to have formed strong alliances with an array of visionary organisations that share our passion for advancing scientific discovery and innovation. Through their generous contributions and support, AIMS RIC has been able to flourish and make groundbreaking strides in various fields. These funding partners enable us to tackle complex challenges and explore new frontiers of knowledge.

At AIMS RIC, we believe in the power of collaboration to drive innovation and impact. Our partners, spanning academic institutions, industry leaders, government bodies, and non-governmental organisations, play a crucial role in advancing our mission. We are joining forces to build a brighter future through research, education, and practical solutions to Africa’s most pressing challenges.
