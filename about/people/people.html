MEET OUR TEAM

AIMS RIC brings together a multidisciplinary team of scholars to lead the development of our priority research programs, supported by a team of administrative staff who play a vital role in facilitating the day-to-day operations of the centre.

Our Governance

AIMS RIC is led by the Management Committee, chaired by the Centre President with support from the Director of Scientific Development and Administration, the Director of Programs Delivery and Reporting, the Academic Manager, and the Finance Manager. The Management Committee plays a crucial role in the governance of the centre, providing strategic leadership and oversight to ensure the centre's mission and objectives are achieved. The chair of this committee works closely with the AIMS Council of Centre Presidents and the AIMS International Governing Board (IGB), which oversees a regular independent financial audit that provides accountability to AIMS’ funders and stakeholders

Research Chairs

Our Senior Researchers hold prestigious positions aimed at recognizing their impact and leadership in their respective fields. Some of their responsibilities include leading research projects, mentoring students and junior faculty, securing funding, building collaborative networks, publishing findings, and contributing to AIMS RIC's strategic vision. Their work is supported by funds and grants from bodies focused on awarding individuals with exceptional contributions to their disciplines.

Resident Researchers

Our Resident Researchers are integral members of the centre because of their dedication to advancing knowledge and innovation within their areas of research. They engage in rigorous research activities, often collaborating with colleagues and students to explore complex questions and develop new theories or applications. Their presence at AIMS RIC enriches the intellectual community, driving forward the centre's mission to foster discovery, learning, and the dissemination of knowledge.

Visiting Researchers

Our Visiting Researchers are esteemed scholars temporarily affiliated with AIMS RIC to collaborate on specific projects and share their expertise. During their stay, these scholars engage in joint research initiatives, often bringing unique perspectives and specialised knowledge that enrich AIMS RIC’s work. They conduct seminars and workshops, fostering intellectual exchange and collaboration among our researchers and students. Their presence helps to build and strengthen international and interdisciplinary networks, enhancing our research capacity and global reputation.

ADMINISTRATIVE TEAM

This is the backbone of our institution! The admin team ensures smooth and efficient operations across all departments. They handle a wide range of responsibilities, including managing budgets, coordinating events, maintaining records, and providing support to visitors, researchers, and students. This team ensures compliance with institutional policies and procedures, facilitates communication within the organisation, and serves as the first point of contact for external partners and visitors.
