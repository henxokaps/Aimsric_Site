ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

AIMS RIC offers a unique atmosphere that prepares research masters and Ph.D. students for careers in academia and industry. Our students are supervised and mentored by professors and leading researchers from across the globe. We are committed to preparing our students to reach the threshold of academic research independence required for knowledge creation and application. To achieve this, we have created a highly motivating and interactive environment for pushing the boundaries of scientific knowledge. This allows our research masters and Ph.D. students to gain high-quality research experience in a rich environment within state-of-the-art facilities and professional career guidance.

We are annually looking for motivated, talented students to become involved in research projects covering areas such as Data, Quantum, and Modeling.

RESEARCH FOCUS

Our research focuses on three interrelated areas: Data, Quantum, and Modeling. Within these broad domains, we delve into specific subareas such as Climate Science, Bayesian Methodology, Climate and Health Interactions, Infectious Disease Modeling, Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Air Pollution, Hydrogen and Clean Fuels, and Quantum Science. These subareas allow us to explore critical intersections and applications of data and quantum principles in addressing complex global challenges.

01
Data

We are committed to developing Artificial intelligence (AI) solutions that benefit humanity while ensuring harmony with the environment, drawing inspiration from Africa's millennia-long tradition of living in balance with nature.

02
Modelling

We are leaders in modeling the climate of Africa and in diagnostics for diseases like COVID-19. We led the development of a highly efficient diagnostic approach that saved the government of Rwanda millions of US dollars in its response to the pandemic.

03
Quantum

We aim to position Africa to harness the vast benefits of quantum revolutions. We have established a research chair in quantum and support several junior scientists whose work centers on quantum exploration.

OUR FUNDERS

AIMS RIC is funded through public-private partnerships which combine government, foundations, corporations, partner institutions, and individuals who share in the goal of unlocking Africa’s scientific potential to foster major discovery. We are grateful to have formed strong alliances with an array of visionary organizations that share our passion for advancing scientific discovery and innovation.
